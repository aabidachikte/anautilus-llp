OUR ORIGIN & PURPOSE

Restoring the Vitality of Our River Ecosystems

Based out of Pune, India, Anautilus LLP combines modular ecological engineering with cutting-edge deployment tools to tackle urban river pollution and industrial waste flow directly at the source.

What is Anautilus LLP?

Anautilus LLP is an environmental engineering firm dedicated to cleaning up and protecting solid-waste channels across India's waterways. Founded and operated in Pune, we serve as a vital link between technological innovation and environmental preservation.

Our operation designs, builds, and maintains custom mechanical barriers and structural waste management catchments that prevent urban plastic pollution from destroying delicate marine habitats downstream.

Our Vision

To establish an unyielding network of clean, plastic-free river channels throughout urban India, setting standard benchmarks for modern ecological engineering and resource recovery.

Our Mission

To eliminate surface-level marine pollutants through localized engineering barriers, automated sorting systems, and collaborative public-private environmental frameworks.

OPERATIONAL STRATEGY

What We Do

Mechanical Interception

We deploy customizable debris barriers designed to safely snare surface plastics without impeding native fish populations or blocking water currents.

Waste Management

Every ton of trash collected is brought ashore and fed into priority sorting systems, ensuring all recyclable components bypass landfills completely.

Ecological Analytics

Our field technicians log daily flow patterns and extraction metrics, offering transparent data sets used for future environmental planning.
