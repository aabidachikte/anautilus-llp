Dahisar River

Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Mumbai

Tracing its origin through pure forest pathways, the Dahisar stream requires spiraled, nautilus-inspired filtration frameworks to intercept urban blockages before they taint the source.

Mithi River

Vihar Lake to Mahim Creek, Mumbai

The heavy structural burden of the Mithi demands resilient, deep-sea engineering tactics. Anautilus aims to clear complex channels, restoring the natural, organic pressure chambers of the riverbed.

Mutha River

Western Ghats to Pune City

Sustaining vast agricultural and urban corridors, the Mutha is an essential chamber of life. Our objective is to guide its stream cleanly, preventing debris from choking its expansion.

Mula River

Mulshi Dam to Pune Confluence

Where the Mula surges, balanced currents must be maintained. By applying the fluid dynamics of ocean-dwelling sentinels, Anautilus optimizes debris extraction without changing natural flow patterns.

Sailing Through Uncharted Paths

Deploying advanced architectural concepts to map, extract, and clean complex waterways with absolute precision. Our tracking technologies ensure data transparency while maintaining the intrinsic ecosystem biodiversity of local rivers from resource to mouth.

A Localized Crisis

Intercepting Waste Across Mumbai & Pune

Every single day, tons of industrial plastic fragments, solid waste, and urban debris choke critical water basins across the Maharashtra region. From the dense city curves of Mumbai’s Mithi River down to the broad agricultural channels of Pune's Mula-Mutha confluence, the damage cascades rapidly toward our oceans.

Anautilus LLP operates at these precise geographic bottlenecks. By deploying custom, non-invasive barrier networks and fluid dynamic extraction systems, we intercept synthetic materials directly from high-velocity currents before they can disintegrate into hazardous microplastics.
